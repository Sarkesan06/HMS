📖 How to Use Video Consultation

1
Book an Online Appointment - When booking, select "Video Consultation" as the type
2
Get Appointment ID - After booking, you'll receive an Appointment ID
3
Join at Scheduled Time - On your appointment date/time, come to this page
4
Enter Appointment ID - Type your Appointment ID and click "Start / Join Call"
5
Allow Camera & Microphone - Grant permission when prompted
6
Start Consultation - You're now in the video call with your doctor!
💡 Pro Tips:
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Use headphones for better audio quality
  • Find a quiet, well-lit room
  • Test your camera and microphone before the call
  • Join 5 minutes before your scheduled time
